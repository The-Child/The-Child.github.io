Rules: Each player can press a button only once per round. Whenever the HP of a player decreases to zero the player loses. Whenever the MP of a player decreases to zero the his or her anti magic or magic powers anymore. The Attack button inflicts 3-6 points of HP damage on the other player. The Magic button inflicts 1-10 points of HP damage on the other player and decreases the Magic power of own player with 3-6 points. The Anti Magic button decreases the magic power and the health power of a sorcerer with 3-6 points and inflicts 10 points Magic damage on the other player. The New Game button resets all the points.
